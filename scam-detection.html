Common Scam Types

Learn to recognize and avoid these common threats

Phishing Emails

Fraudulent emails designed to steal personal information or credentials.

HIGH RISK
  • • Urgent requests for action
  • • Suspicious sender addresses
  • • Requests for passwords

Phone Scams

Fraudulent calls impersonating legitimate organizations or authorities.

HIGH RISK
  • • IRS/Government impersonation
  • • Tech support scams
  • • Prize/lottery scams

Crypto Scams

Fraudulent cryptocurrency investments and fake wallet schemes.

HIGH RISK
  • • Fake investment platforms
  • • Ponzi schemes
  • • Wallet draining

Romance Scams

Fake online relationships designed to extract money from victims.

MEDIUM RISK
  • • Quick declarations of love
  • • Requests for money
  • • Avoidance of video calls

Shopping Scams

Fake online stores and fraudulent marketplace listings.

MEDIUM RISK
  • • Too-good-to-be-true prices
  • • No secure payment options
  • • Poor website quality

Identity Theft

Unauthorized use of personal information for fraudulent purposes.

HIGH RISK
  • • Data breaches
  • • Social engineering
  • • Document theft
